Random Facts

  1. I am a computer scientist and jazz musician.
  2. I enjoy analyzing and designing systems like math, music, and languages.
  3. Some of my favorite musicians (aside from my parents) are Mike Manieri, Stefon Harris, Bill Evans, Chick Corea, Jaco Pastorius, Rocco Prestia, Aretha Franklin, and James Brown.
  4. A few of my favorite bands are Tower of Power, Earth Wind and Fire, Gordon Goodwin's Big Phat Band, and Return to Forever.
  5. Some of my favorite jazz standards are Nardis, A Night In Tunisia, Bernie's Tune, Samba De Orfeu, and Beautiful Love.
  6. Though I am a vibraphonist, those are not my arms on the left. They are Joe Locke's.
  7. My other hobbies include playing and watching baseball, composing and arranging music, skiing, and reading.
  8. Though I have interests in music and computer science, I unfortunately am not the Alex Strong who, with Kevin Karplus, invented an algorithm to synthesize plucked strings and percussion and published a paper in 1983.
  9. Having grown up in Manhattan, I am a Yankees fan.
  10. Ernie Harwell was my seventh cousin, three times removed.
  11. Sometimes, I like to take nice long bike rides by the Hudson River or over the George Washington Bridge.
  12. I also like problem solving and working on projects like this website, the first one I ever made (mostly) from scratch.
  13. I've studied Latin for 5 years (most recently in fall 2014) and won a dictionary from the National Latin Exam in 2013.
  14. I was born in New Hampshire, but my family moved to New York City when I was two years old.
